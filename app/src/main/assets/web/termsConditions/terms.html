1. Introduction

Nikshay Patra- Hypro is a food delivery service designed to provide meals to patients under the National Tuberculosis Elimination Program (NTEP). The App utilizes geo-tagging technology to ensure the accurate delivery of meals.

The App allows users to order meals for the patient and track the delivery.

Meals are delivered to patients based on their eligibility and location.

Geo-tagging is used to confirm the delivery to the correct patient at the designated location.
